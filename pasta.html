Directions (Checklist)

  1. Preheat oven to 400.
  2. Add 1/2 cup olive oil to a baking dish and toss with whole cherry tomatoes. Salt and pepper until everything is coated.
  3. Place the whole block of feta in the middle of the pan and top with a splash more of olive oil plus a few cranks of fresh pepper.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare bowtie pasta according to directions then strain.
  6. After the 30 minutes has passed, crank the heat up to 450 and bake for another 5-10 minutes or until the feta and tomatoes have browned.
  7. Remove the baking dish from the oven and add garlic and red pepper flakes to taste.
  8. Stir so the residual heat cooks the garlic and releases the flavors from the red pepper flakes.
  9. Toss in the pasta and stir one more time.
  10. Finish with fresh basil, another splash of olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  11. If the dish is perfectly creamy, then it’s ready to serve! If it’s a little dry, add 1/4 cup pasta water and stir it up.
